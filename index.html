Tracking is fast and simple.

Verbalizing your emotions can be difficult, so we’ve made the process simple. Capture your mood and energy in a couple of swipes. Add an optional text entry and your location to give your data relevant context.

Visualize your health.

Your input creates a graph that’s easy to read. Record your mood and energy multiple days in a row to reveal a trend. Tracking multiple times in a day creates a daily average.

Journaling is an important act of self-reflection. Take a moment each day to record your emotions and thoughts.
