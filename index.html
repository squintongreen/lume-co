Tracking is fun and easy.

Collect data about your mood and energy with minimal effort. Add a text entry to give your data context.

Home is wherever you are.

Your home screen is a snapshot of your mood and energy. The heads up display shows your daily and weekly average, your active goals, and entry count.

Visualize your health.

Compare your mood and energy with an easily readable graph. See how different aspects of your health are related.

Days at a glance and in detail.

Your daily entries are displayed in a readable format. Entries for each day are a tap away.

A support system for success.

Lume is a system of interlocking parts, one supporting the other.

Set goals, stay motivated.

By committing your goals to writing, you greatly increase your chances of acheiving success.

Give your data meaning.

Use the Journal to fill in the details. Feeling down? Write about it. Had an amazing day? Take the time to reflect on why.

Modular by design.

Lume is extensible. New ways to track your health will provide you with new methods of visualizing your health.
