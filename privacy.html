Lume Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to the Lume (“Lume”) website and application (“Site” and “App”). Thank you for choosing to use our Service and our App. We try to make our Privacy Policy easy to understand so that you are informed as to how we use your information. This Privacy Policy, like our Terms of Service, is an integral part of using our Service; therefore you must completely agree to our Privacy Policy in order to use our Site or Service. You must be over the age of 13 to use our Site, Service or App.

Information Collected

Personally Identifiable Information Submitted by You

You will not be required to provide us any information when you visit our Site. However, in order to use our Service and access our App, we will collect your name, address, date of birth, and email.

Non-Identifying Information

Whenever you use our Site or Service, we may collect non-identifying information from you, such as your IP address, interactions with the Site and Service, query information, location, referring URL, browser, operating system, cookie information, UID, geo location data, usage, data transferred, and Internet Service Provider. Additionally, we may collect information regarding your usage and interaction with our App or Site. Without a subpoena, voluntary compliance on the part of your Internet Service Provider, or additional records from a third party such as your wireless provider, this information alone cannot usually be used to identify you.

Health Care Information Collected

Whenever you use our App or Site we may collect additional information about your health. This information includes information related to past, present or future physical, mood, or mental health or conditions of you as an individual. Additionally, we may collect information regarding your usage and interaction with our App.

Use of Your Information

We will never sell or transfer any personally identifiable information to third parties without your consent. However, you agree that we may use your information:

  • To enhance or improve our users’ experiences.
  • To provide our Service to you.
  • To email you newsletters and correspondence.
  • To contact you and to respond to inquiries.
  • To understand how you use our App.
  • To transfer or sell to a third party with your permission.

Additionally, we may give your information to law enforcement if we are compelled to by a court order, if there has been a violation of any US laws or if a violation of the Terms of Service or Privacy Policy has occurred.

Accessing, Editing, and Removing Your Information

When using our App or Site we may collect personal and non-personal information from you. If you wish to opt out of such collection please contact us. If you have any questions or wish to review, change, or access any of your information collected by us, please contact us. Additionally, if you would like us to remove any of your information, please contact us. After you discontinue your use of our Site or App we may keep inaccessible copies of your information for a commercially reasonable period of time for internal and legal purposes.

Cookies

We use cookies to remember your preferences for this reason, you must have cookies enabled in your browser to use our Site and Services. Additionally, some or all of our cookies may be accessible by third parties.
