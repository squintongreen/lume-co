Senior iOS Developer

Atlanta, GA, United States

Apply Now

We're a small company with a big ambition: to help people be amazing. By better leveraging personal data our goal is to empower individuals to make decisions with better outcomes. We are looking for the help of a talented and experienced senior developer. A love of problem solving and a creative approach to overcoming obstacles are a few of the key qualities you should possess. We are looking for a developer who knows best practices and has a deep understanding of iOS; while you understand the importance of design patterns and convention, you aren't beholden to doing something simply because "that's the way it's always been done".

Responsibilities

  • Lead a small iOS development team
  • Uses technical expertise to architect solutions, write code, and performs testing and debugging of the application
  • Establish timelines for development of product features
  • Contributes to documentation and works directly with the team to develop and instill best practices
  • Identifying current and potential pain points within the application code
  • May interact with users to define system requirements and/or necessary modifications
  • Ability to work directly with management to build the product
  • Provides leadership and guidance to others

Required Skills

  • 3+ years of iOS development experience
  • Have published two or more apps in the iOS App Store
  • Strong planning, communication and troubleshooting skills
  • Strong problem solving skills
  • You display an affinity for the details, but won't let perfectionism stand in the way of getting things done
  • Deep knowledge of Objective-C, object oriented programming, and data structures
  • Experience working with SQLite
  • Experience with XIBs
  • Solid understanding of full mobile application development life-cycle
  • Working knowledge of the mobile landscape, trends and emerging technologies

Bonus Round

  • A working knowledge of C#
  • Experience with Xamarin
  • Some experience leading a team or in a Project Management role

Education

  • BS in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or a degree in a comparable field
