Product Marketing Specialist

Atlanta, GA, United States

Apply now

We may be new on the quantified self scene, but we’re no strangers to the impact that a consistent and engaging marketing message can have. We’re looking for a creative and driven Product Marketing Specialist to help us craft an engaging narrative centered around personal health and the benefits of living a quantified life. Our goal is to change the way individuals view their personal health, and we need your help to get there. You’d be at the forefront of our efforts and be directly responsible for decisions that shape our message. You’ll work with our engineers and product designer to set positioning and design a strategy that will drive Lume’s growth in key markets. You’ll use your full range of skills from market research, copywriting, and social media savvy to help us broadcast our story with words, images, and data. With the ability to strongly impact both the company and the industry, product marketing specialists in this role must have the skills to succeed in a fast-paced startup environment and to execute independently on a variety of tasks.

Responsibilities

  • Extend Lume brand via compelling copy and an engaging presence on social media outlets
    • Twitter: Wade through the stream and join/start conversations around quantified self
    • Facebook: Interact with and engage individuals on a deeper, more personal level.
    • Google+: Leverage the Communities feature to gain an active and involved audience.
  • Manage day-to-day activities on various media outlets. Since you’re amazing at what you do (and love it too!), you’ll be ready to roll with a posting schedule, keeping our message consistent in timing and content.
  • Help us keep current users engaged, and expose to them the benefits of regularly tracking their health.
  • Manage execution of marketing tactics to support strategies
    • social media (Twitter, Facebook, Google+)
    • blog articles
    • newsletters
    • landing page

Required

  • A deep passion around something in your life: films, books, Psych, lanyards, etc.
  • Excellent verbal and written communication skills. As a product marketing specialist, your wit and your pen are your primary tools of choice, and you are extremely skilled in wielding both.
  • Project management skills are neccessary, as you will be running campaigns on multiple platforms. You must be able to prioritize high-impact activities and keep complicated projects moving forward.
  • Analytical skills to analyze research findings and user data, and you should be able to implement appropriate tracking to measure success.
  • An understanding of brand development, including voice and visual branding.
  • A deep love for helping individuals and solving tough problems.
  • Must love puns.
  • And coffee.
  • 2-3 years of Product Marketing experience. This includes knowledge of marketing at different parts of a product life-cycle: new product, iterative releases of existing products, and large/mature products

OR

  • Bachelor’s Degree in Marketing or related field

One million bonus points if you...

  • Have experience with MailChimp.
    • creating and managing email campaigns
    • creating and managing email lists
  • Enjoy conducting market research to identify gaps in market fit, and segments for possible future expansion.
  • Are able to leverage market research to craft compelling campaigns which effectively deliver message to potential new users, driving user growth.
  • Are driven to get to know the competitive market landscape and our users. Remember, we’re not just here to sell - our mission is to help people gain a deeper understanding of themselves.

Please include a cover letter and attach your resume.
