Brand Designer

Atlanta, GA, United States

Apply now

We may be new on the quantified self scene, but we’re no strangers to the impact that a consistent and engaging branding message can have. We’re looking for a driven Brand Designer to help us craft a strong brand and compelling visual language to compliment our grand ambitions. Our goal is to change the way individuals view their personal health, and we need your help to get there. You’d be at the forefront of our efforts and be directly responsible for decisions that shape our message. You’ll use your full range of skills from graphic design, illustration, copywriting, and social media savvy to help us craft our story - be it with images, words or data.

Responsibilities

  • You are actively involved in defining product strategy.
  • Help develop the Lume brand and identity through design, illustration, copy writing and data visualization
  • Collaborate with product designers and engineers to communicate the value of personal data collection
  • Identify opportunities to impact the brand, and developing a creative strategy to support your ideas
  • You obsess over the deets (also known as details), and are no stranger to gently massaging pixels into place.
  • Manage execution of marketing tactics to support strategies
    • social media (Twitter, Facebook, Google+)
    • blog articles
    • newsletters
    • landing page
  • Develop needed assets to execute various campaigns
    • print
    • web/mobile
    • digital
  • Understand our users and the competitive market landscape, which includes getting your hands dirty in market research
  • Challenge design conventions while maintaining focus

Required Skills

  • A deep passion around something in your life: music, books, POGs, Knolling, etc.
  • Excellent communication skills. You should be able to articulate your ideas and design decisions in a clear manner, and what problems they’re solving.
  • Project management skills, as you will be running campaigns on multiple platforms. During major product updates, you may be required to run multiple campaigns simultaneously.
  • You love making beautiful stuff and you have a strong understanding of composition, color use, balance, symmetry, and white-space.
  • You are comfortable working within and expanding the ‘Lume style’.
  • You’re a self-starter who actively seeks ways to make self tracking appealing to people who have no idea what “quantified self” means.
  • Photoshop and Illustrator are our best friends here at Lume. You three should be beyond comfortable and into the best friend phase.
  • A deep love for helping individuals and solving tough problems.
  • An appreciation of how good design can help improve people's lives.
  • Must love or hate puns. Either way, you better not be middle of the road!
  • Will read everything and bring a an unopened pack of M&M’s with all of the orange ones removed.
  • You want to work on a team of super-talented engineers and designers to help challenge the way the word thinks about personal health and wellness!
  • 3-5 years of experience (either university or equivalent job experience).
  • Pandas!

One million bonus points if you...

  • Love using MailChimp. This includes:
    • creating and managing email campaigns
    • creating and managing email lists
  • Have a solid understanding of core web technologies such as...
    • HTML
    • CSS
    • Web Standards
    • Responsive design

Please include a link to your portfolio and attach your resume.
